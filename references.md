1. **Ronald L. Graham, Donald E. Knuth, Oren Patashnik, Concrete Mathematics (2nd Edition).**
   - Comprehensive introduction to permutations, combinations, and their applications.

2. **Richard P. Stanley, Enumerative Combinatorics.**
   - In-depth exploration of combinatorial mathematics, including permutations and combinations.

3. **Wikipedia: Permutation**
   - https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Permutation
   - Theory, formulas, and examples of permutations.

4. **Wikipedia: Combination**
   - https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Combination
   - Theory and examples of combinations.

5. **NPTEL: Combinatorics**
   - https://nptel.ac.in/courses/111/106/111106112/
   - Video lectures and notes on permutations and combinations.
